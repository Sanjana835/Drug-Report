From addiction to recovery: A journey of hope and resilience


This is a story of transformation, where darkness gave way to light. Through unwavering determination and support, a path was carved from the depths of addiction to the heights of recovery. This journey exemplifies the power of hope and resilience.



Background: Anil, a construction worker from Mumbai, became addicted to fentanyl, a powerful synthetic opioid, after it was illegally distributed in his community. His addiction led to severe physical and mental health problems, including near-fatal overdoses.


Rehabilitation: Following multiple overdoses, Anil’s family admitted him to a rehabilitation center. He underwent medical detoxification and received treatment through opioid substitution therapy (OST) and counseling. Anil also participated in group therapy and family counseling to rebuild his support system. He has maintained sobriety for two years and is now involved in community outreach programs to raise awareness about the dangers of fentanyl.


Anil’s Fentanyl Addiction

Background: Ravi, a college student in Delhi, became addicted to Gamma-Hydroxybutyrate (GHB), a drug known for its depressant effects. His addiction led to impaired cognitive function, academic failure, and legal issues.


Rehabilitation: Ravi’s addiction was discovered after a series of legal and academic troubles. He entered a rehabilitation center where he received detoxification, cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT), and life skills training. The program also included recreational therapy and stress management techniques. Ravi has been in recovery for a year and is actively involved in peer support groups and drug prevention initiatives.


Ravi’s GHB Addiction

Background:Kiran, an artist from Goa, began using LSD (acid) for its hallucinogenic effects, which he felt enhanced his creativity. Over time, his use became compulsive, leading to severe psychological issues and disruptions in his personal and professional life.


Rehabilitation: After a psychotic episode, Kiran sought help and entered a rehabilitation center in Goa. His treatment included detoxification, individual therapy, and cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT). The center also provided art therapy, which helped him reconnect with his passion for art in a healthier way. Kiran has been clean for over a year and now helps others through art therapy and drug prevention workshops.


Kiran’s LSD Addiction

Background: Rajesh, a software engineer from Hyderabad, started using crack cocaine during high-stress periods at work. What began as occasional use quickly developed into a severe addiction that impacted his health, career, and personal life.


Rehabilitation: After a series of health crises and a breakdown at work, Rajesh’s family arranged for him to enter a rehabilitation center.
